Who We Are

Fast bikes. Bigger mission.

Team Fun is a cycling club built on two things — an inviting environment where everyone belongs, and a genuine commitment to making the world better. Every rider gives back.

We're proud partners of GiveDirectly — an organization that sends cash directly to the world's poorest households, no strings attached. No overhead theater, no paternalism. Just money, going to families who decide how best to spend it.

Team Fun riders help accelerate the end of extreme poverty. When we race, we raise. When we ride, we give. Every mile adds up to real change for families that need it most.
